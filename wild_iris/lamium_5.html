Lamium

Lamium (page 5): "This is how you live when you have a cold heart."

Other names: Dead nettles

Family: Lamiaceae

Type: Herbaceous mostly semi-evergreen annual or perennial

Blooming period: Spring to Summer

Sun requirement: Full or partial shade

Soil requirement: Moist but well-drained.

Notes: The leaves of some varieties of lamium have their interiors marked with silver. Somtimes considered a weed; sometimes used for groundcover.
