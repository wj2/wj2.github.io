Tulip

The Doorway (page 33): "the grass not yet / high at the edge of the garden, the early tulips / beginning to open--"

Family: Liliaceae

Type: Herbaceous perennial bulb

Blooming period: Spring

Sun requirement: Sun

Soil requirement: Well-drained

Notes: Tulips do not produce nectar, and rely on wind and land animals for pollen distribution.
