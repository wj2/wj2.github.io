Witchgrass

Witchgrass (page 22): Witchgrass: "Something / comes into the world unwelcome / calling disorder, disorder"

Other names: Panicum capillare

Family: Poaceae

Type: Annual herbaceous grass

Blooming period: Summer

Soil requirement: Dry, sandy, waste places, cultivated ground

https: //soilcropandmore.info/crops/Grasses/Witchgrass/Witchgrass-Panicum-capillare.htm

Notes: Widely considered a weed, witchgrass flowers release seeds that can travel far on the wind.
