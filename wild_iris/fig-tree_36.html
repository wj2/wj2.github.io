Fig Tree

Vespers (page 36): "Once I believed in you; I planted a fig tree."

Other names: Ficus carica

Family: Moraceae

Type: Evergreen or deciduous tree

Blooming period: Spring to Summer

Sun requirement: Full sun or partial shade.

Soil requirement: Fertile, well-drained.

Notes: Fig fruit is in season from late summer to early fall. Fresh figs do not keep well and thus are difficult to transport. The tree tolerates only moderate seasonal frost.
