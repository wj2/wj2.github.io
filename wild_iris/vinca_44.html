Vinca

Vespers (page 44): "I remember the sunlight of early spring, embankments / netted with dark vinca."

Other names: Periwinkle

Family: Apocynaceae

Type: Herbaceous or woody (subshrub) evergreen perennial

Blooming period: Spring to early Fall

Sun requirement: Partial shade, shade

Soil requirement: Any

Notes: Can be used as a groundcover in full shade, but will flower more with sun.

Sources: [1],[14]
