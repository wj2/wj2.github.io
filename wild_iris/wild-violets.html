Wild Violets

Family: Violaceae

Type: Herbaceous semi evergreen perennial .

Blooming period: late Winter to early Spring

Sun requirement: Sun or partial shade.

Soil requirement: Rock gardens, screes, and alpine houses. Moist, well-drained soil.

Notes: The flowers are violet or white.
