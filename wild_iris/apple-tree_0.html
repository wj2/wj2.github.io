Apple Tree

Family: Rosaceae

Type: Deciduous tree

Blooming period: Spring

Sun requirement: Sun, can tolerate partial shade

Soil requirement: Any but waterlogged

Notes: In addition to their flowers and fruit, apple trees also have notable fall color.
