Daisies

Daisies (page 39): "Go ahead: say what you're thinking. The garden / is not the real world."

Family: Asteraceae

Type: Herbaceous perennial or biennial

Blooming period: Spring

Sun requirement: Full sun or partial-shade.

Soil requirement: Fertile, very well-drained.

Notes: The flower consists of white rays arranged around a yellow center. Daisies grow widely, and are sometimes considered a weed.
