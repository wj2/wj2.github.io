Blueberries

Family: Ericaceae

Type: Deciduous or evergreen woody (prostrate shrubs ) perennial

Blooming period: late Spring to early Summer

Sun requirement: Sun, partial shade

Soil requirement: Moist, well-drained, peaty or sandy, acidic

Notes: Blueberry plants are categorized into highbush and lowbush; highbush is typically cultivated and lowbush is typically wild. The berries follow the flowers.
