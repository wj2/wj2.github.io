A botanical companion to The Wild Iris

This zine collects images of and some basic information about all the plants mentioned in Louise Gl√ºck's book The Wild Iris (please contact me if you notice any that are missing). The zine is meant as a reference for the curious and -- like me -- botanically ignorant reader of this wonderful book.

There is a pdf version of the zine available here.

Practical notes: There is a navigation bar at the top of each page. Hover over italicized words in "Type" section to see the definition from the glossary. Hover over the number(s) in the "References" section to see the reference.
