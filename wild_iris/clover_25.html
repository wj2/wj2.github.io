Clover

Matins (page 25): "on my knees, pulling / clumps of clover from the flower beds"

Family: Fabaceae

Type: Herbaceous, deciduous or semievergreen annual, biennial, or short-lived perennial

Blooming period: late Spring to Summer

Sun requirement: Full sun.

Soil requirement: Well-drained.

Notes: 3-lobed leaves and heads of pealike flowers in a variety of colors.
