Pine

The Wild Iris (page 1): "Overhead, noises, branches of the pine shifting."

Family: Pinaceae

Type: Coniferous, evergreen tree

Sun requirement: Full sun

Soil requirement: Acidic, mostly well-drained

Notes: The cones produced by pines can ripen over multiple years, from green to brown. The pinaceae family includes pines, firs, cedars, hemlocks, larches, and spruces.
