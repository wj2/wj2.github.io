Peas

The Garden (page 16): "the young couple planting / a row of peas, as though / no one has ever done this before"

Family: Fabaceae

Type: Herbaceous annual

Notes: Pea plants reach maturity about sixty days after planting. Peas do not thrive in the heat of summer.
