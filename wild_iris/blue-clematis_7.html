Blue Clematis

Clear Morning (page 7): "tendrils / of blue clematis, light // of early evening"

Family: Ranunculaceae

Type: Herbaceous or woody, evergreen or deciduous, perennial vine

Blooming period: different cultivars flower from late Winter to Fall

Sun requirement: Sun or partial shade.

Soil requirement: Rich, well-drained soil, shaded roots.

Notes: The color of a clematis depends on the climate: warmer climates are associated with darker flowers.
