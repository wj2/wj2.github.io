The Hawthorn Tree

Other names: Crataegus

Family: Rosaceae

Type: Deciduous (or rarely semi-evergreen) tree

Blooming period: Spring to Summer

Sun requirement: Full sun is best.

Soil requirement: Any but very wet.

Notes: The tree blooms widely, often with white flowers -- and also often shows fall colors later in the year.
