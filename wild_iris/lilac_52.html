Lilac

Vespers (page 52): "when I close my eyes, / birdsong, scent of lilac in early spring, scent of summer roses"

Other names: Syringa

Family: Oleaceae

Type: Woody deciduous perennial (small tree)

Blooming period: Spring to early Summer

Sun requirement: Sun

Soil requirement: Deep, fertile, well-drained, alkaline

Notes: The flowers are often very fragrant.
