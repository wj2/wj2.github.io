Peonies

Presque Isle (page 49): "Muslin, flicker of silver. Heavy jar filled with white peonies."

Family: Paeoniaceae

Type: Herbaceous or woody, deciduous perennial

Blooming period: late Spring

Sun requirement: Sun, partial shade

Soil requirement: Rich, well-drained

Notes: The foliage has rich fall color.
