Silver Lily

Vespers (page 56): "stands of late lilies—optimism / of the great stalks—imperial gold and silver"

Family: Liliaceae

Type: Herbaceous perennial bulb

Blooming period: Summer

Sun requirement: Full sun.

Soil requirement: Well-drained.

Notes: There are many different varieties of lily, flowering in many different colors.
