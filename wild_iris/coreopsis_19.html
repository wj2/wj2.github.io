Coreopsis

Love in Moonlight (page 19): "a whole world / thrown away on the moon: ... the narrow garden / where the cat hides, rolling on its back in the dust, / the rose, the coreopsis"

Family: Asteraceae

Type: Herbaceous annuals and perennials

Blooming period: Summer

Sun requirement: Sun

Soil requirement: Fertile, well-drained

Notes: The flowers often resemble daisies.
