Crocus

The Garden (page 16): "in thin grass, bursts of purple crocus"

Family: Iridaceae

Type: Herbaceous perennial

Blooming period: late Winter to early Spring, some Fall

Sun requirement: Sun

Soil requirement: Well-drained

Notes: The crocus is acaulescent (lacking a visible above ground stem) and grows from corms (an underground storage organ).
